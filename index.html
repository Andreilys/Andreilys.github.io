About Me

Mission: To use data science and machine learning to filter the signal out of the noise and improve our personal decision making



Rising senior studying Computer Science, interested in data science and machine learning. Background working at two venture-backed high growth startups in Los Angeles as well as international experience in China working for a Fortune 500. Additional experience starting and running a business with 8 full-time employees. Written work has appeared in Forbes, Huffington Post, and Startup Grind.
Working as a data scientist intern I've had the opportunity to build:

  • Machine learning models with Python
  • Time series forecasts using Prophet
  • Actionable SQL dashboards in Periscope
  • ETL pipelines using Airflow
  • Dimension and fact tables in Postgres
  • KPI design and SQL reports to track performance


Throughout my experience, I've also had the opportunity to engage with a variety of business stakeholders focused on introducing new processes as a result of insights extracted from deep analysis. Additionally, I've lead key data initiatives within organizations in the form of SQL workshops, office hours and tech talks to help organizations become more data driven & data literate.
